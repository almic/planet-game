# Cutscene
- Heavy use of spacing between lines, camera shots of the scene before something important happens
- Linger on things a bit more. Don't cut shots just because there's no dialogue, expression, etc. Fill.

# Exposition
- The MC must NOT be trained on the job, or given important mission details half-way through the game (with the exception of the secret item recovery). Never let it be asked by the player "Why would I not already know about that? You sent me here for a job and waited to tell me something important until the last second?" As an example, the photo mission, don't tell the player on a radio "by the way we packed a camera in your bag so take the picture now" it needs to be established well before that point.
