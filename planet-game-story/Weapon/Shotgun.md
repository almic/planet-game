Heavy weapon, shotgun, really good at stunning and destroying machines, large and small.

# Function
Excels at stunning and destroying machines. Cannot penetrate armor, but spread shot impacts are able to damage machine internals. Can destroy smaller machines easily. Ammunition is carried on planet. Some machines carry it.
