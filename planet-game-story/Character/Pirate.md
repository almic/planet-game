The motivating incident, the reason the [[MC]] is sent to the surface of the planet.  A group of pirates were stealing a weapon from the [[Weapon Facility]] on the day before [[World Break]].

# Theories
Here are some possibilities for what happened to the pirates.
- Primary escape vehicle damaged, broke down before reaching the [[Ship Platform]]. The pirates missed their window.
- Encountered rogue bots, everyone killed, vehicle destroyed.
- One member survived, leaving "landmines" in the story for the [[MC]] to fix. They sabotaged the [[Superdeep Borehole Facility]], reactivated the mega bot, etc.
- Surviving member did not survive the mega bot, or died from lack of supplies somewhere. If there is a surviving member, player must encounter their body.

# Why does he fight the MC?
Initially the pirate expected to see a combat team, and use the robots to take hostages, then travel off planet with the hostages. Upon seeing the [[MC]], an armored soldier, the plan changes to kill them, take the suit, and use that to leave the planet.

When that plan fails, he makes a deal with the MC to spare him in return for the location of the weapon.

# Death
When the weapon is retrieved, and body counts confirmed, the pirate is told to travel to the [[Ship Platform]] and await further instructions. Once he leaves the compound, he is spotted by satellites and destroyed.
