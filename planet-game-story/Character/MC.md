Who is the MC?
A contracted soldier with an advanced combat suit. He is one of the guys you send when you want a job done right.

What is the MC doing?
He is part of the final procedure before [[World Break]]. There are a few odd tasks mixed in with the preparations. One job is to go to a natural location to take a photo, for publicity stuff or something. Another is to retrieve a sword left at the [[Weapon Facility]].

Where is the MC from?
He is from a different place. There is no connection to the planet, or really anyone involved. Born to a mining world, interested in rocks and geology forever.

What are the MC's qualifications for the mission?
A lot of combat experience, probably some armed conflicts with people. Definitely an accomplished robotics engineer, particularly with mining machines. Interested in machines since childhood, joined a defense company late teens/ early 20s.

How old is the MC?
At least 25, no older than 28. Definitely younger than [[Mission Leader]], even by a few days.
