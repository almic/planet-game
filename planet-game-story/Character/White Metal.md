Unnamed female who guides the [[MC]] during the special recovery mission at the [[Weapon Facility]]. They are called "White Metal" by the [[Mission Leader]], a term for in-experienced, non-combatants who insert themselves into military operations. The phrase uses "White" to relate the "cleanness" of the individual and "metal" to relate to someone's first time putting on armor.

They are curious about the [[MC]]'s thoughts on the [[Ultra Weapon]], showing they know about the device and have an interest in gathering opinions from soldiers. They mention the [[MC]] is the first to ever use the weapon in a "real combat scenario."

She refuses to give her name initially, prompting the [[Mission Leader]] to openly refer to her as "White Metal." The two are not actually in the same room, communication is by voice only.

### Qualifications
White Metal is actually a senior manager from the [[Weapon Facility]], closely involved in site operations, especially the weapons testing program. This information is not revealed in the game, only hinted at by the tone of their voice and the questions they ask to the [[MC]].

### How Old?
The oldest character in the game. At least older than the [[Pirate]].
Range: 37-50.
