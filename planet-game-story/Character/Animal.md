There is a small animal that follows the [[MC]] closely, at their feet, like a pet.

It does not want to harm the MC, and does not feel threatened by them. It is totally passive. The animal is supposed to represent the player's perspective of the [[MC]] and the events of the game. It is highly intelligent compared to most animals on Earth.

# DO NOT DO THIS
It should not be made to appear cute, stupid, helpless, or animalistic. The animal CANNOT exert force or changes in the game; it cannot break anything, it cannot help the player, it cannot drive the story forward. It also cannot be an obstacle, there must be no task to help the animal. Do not feed the animal. If the animal eats, it appears with food in its mouth. If the animal is in danger, it escapes the danger by itself. If the animal needs help, it helps itself, or it is ignored.

# First Sign
Very early in the game the animal and [[MC]] encounter each other. This can be a setup of something more dangerous than a machine, an intelligent and cunning creature hunting the player.

# Night
One night the [[MC]] is sleeping and the animal wakes up the MC with an animal call. Shot is a night sky with some celestial body that the animal is watching. Could be very dog, so perhaps the call needs to be less howl and something more alien.

# Last Scene
The [[MC]] forces a separation from the animal, such as a command to stay, or some sort of confinement. Potentially makes a "home" or something that the animal is willing to remain at, anticipating the [[MC]] to return later.

Later on, the animal hears the distant launch of the rocket and looks to it and does an animal call. It is the final perspective from the surface of the planet.
