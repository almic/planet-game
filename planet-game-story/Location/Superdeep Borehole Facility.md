One of hundreds of repurposed locations used for [[World Break]]. Ancient facility used for planet crust analysis, then for oil extraction, deep mining, now for charge placement. Charges have already been prepared and placed in the borehole, [[MC]] is just pressing the button.

### Why are there [[Hostile robots]]?
The [[Researcher]] was "inspired" by the encounter with the rogue bots and enables local defensive bots, and hacks simpler machines to attack the [[MC]]. He did this to initially capture hostages, anticipating a "lighter" response. The plan changes to activating the [[Giant Deadly Robot]] upon seeing the [[MC]] arrive.
