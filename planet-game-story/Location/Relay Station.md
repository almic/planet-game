Data collection, analysis, and transmission station. Used to monitor facilities around the area. There are many of these across the planet. This one is primarily used to monitor several nearby Charge sites, including the [[Superdeep Borehole Facility]].

### Why are there [[Hostile robots]] here?
The [[Researcher]] remotely connected to the facility and hacked local defense and random machines. These are particularly aggressive, as they were not intended for protection of the [[Researcher]].
