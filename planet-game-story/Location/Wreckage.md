The wreck of the vehicle used by the [[Researcher|pirates]] to steal the [[Ultra Weapon]]. It is somewhere between the [[Weapon Facility]] and the [[Ship Platform]].

### Why are there [[Hostile robots]]?
The group encountered rogue bots. These were old, defective military bots made by another organization. The noise attracted more from nearby after the initial fight. When the [[Ultra Weapon]] is recovered, [[White Metal]] sends a few special defense robots to prompt [[MC]] to use the weapon.
