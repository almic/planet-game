###### No Deal Scene


# Sequence

During gameplay, after clearing a particularly difficult area of strong bots, [[Mission Leader]] opens comms.

[[Mission Leader]]: "This level of resistance wasn't part of the mission plan, operative, we're pulling you out."

Control is taken from the player

[[MC]] starts to turn around to go back the other way

[[White Metal]] says "No, you can't leave now, we're so close to the objective!"

[[Mission Leader]] says "[[White Metal]], we're done."

[[MC]] is still walking back to the entrance of the room.

[[White Metal]] says "I'll double the payment!"

[[MC]] continues walking to the entrance of the room.

[[Mission Leader]] simply states "No deal."

[[White Metal]] adds "And... we'll let you keep it, if you promise to use it."

[[MC]] stops and waits, [[Mission Leader]] asks "Keep what."

[[White Metal]] clarifies "Your objective, [[The Sword|a sword]]."

There is a short pause, [[MC]] shifts a little.

[[Mission Leader]] asks "What kind of sword?"

[[White Metal]] states "One that goes through metal like no other."

[[Mission Leader]] states "My operative doesn't know how to use a sword."
He knows he is lying, almost giving it away with a slight tone of sarcasm.

[[White Metal]] says "You don't know? Of course he knows how to use a sword! He trained in (some very specific detail). It says so on his profile."

[[MC]] says "You researched me." He is not really asking a question, he is making a statement.

[[White Metal]] asks "Will you do it?"

[[MC]] thinks about it, holding his weapon in a relaxed manner.

[[MC]] says "Yes."

[[White Metal]] says "That's good to hear, you will love it," as [[MC]] turns around and walks off screen.

Gameplay resumes at the exit to the room, where the [[MC]] would be to continue the mission.
