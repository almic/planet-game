###### Sequence showing the [[Pirate]] being destroyed once they leave the [[Superdeep Borehole Facility]]

# Sequence

MC confirms obtaining the weapon, body count at the location.

Listening to mission control, "prepare for fireworks"

MC walks to the opposite side of the vehicle, Animal following closely.

"Target leaving facility"

"Target has entered the vehicle"

MC turns to face the direction of the [[Superdeep Borehole Facility]], the top is visible. Slings weapon over shoulder. Animal sits next to MC.

"Safe distance reached, fire the shot"

A streak flies from space and impacts the ground, obscured by terrain between the MC and the point of impact

The sky glows red, then the top of a fireball can be seen over the obscuring terrain.

"Good splash"

MC turns and walks off camera, animal stays for a moment longer, then turns and walks off towards the MC.
