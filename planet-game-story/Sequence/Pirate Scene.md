###### Pirate encounter where a deal is made

# Sequence

The comes down, revealing a large underground chasm.

[[MC]] steps out of the elevator, the [[Animal]] runs out and off the side of the camera. An illuminated tower is visible, with a window that shows a room with desks and chairs.

[[Researcher]] appears behind the blast-proof glass and says "Hello" to [[MC]]

[[MC]] turns and fires two accurate shots at the glass, to no effect

[[Researcher]] flinches and ducks, says "wait wait! if you try to kill me, the charges will go off!"

[[Mission Leader]] quietly whispers to [[MC]] "Steady". [[MC]] slightly relaxes, and calmly asks "Where is the weapon?"

[[Researcher]] responds "what?", still cowering behind harder cover

[[MC]] repeats the question, a little louder and slightly slower, adding "I won't try to kill you" but keeping his weapon aimed at the glass

[[Researcher]] slowly emerges, asking "You didn't hit the vehicle?"

[[MC]] firmly asks "What vehicle?", not moving a muscle

[[Researcher]] is silent for a moment, looking down, possibly confused by the question.

[[MC]] repeats the question, louder and slower

[[Researcher]] goes "uhh," slightly panicked, trying to think quickly, then says "It isn't here, the weapon, I can tell you where it is if you let me go."

[[MC]] maintains his posture, not responding. He is waiting for [[Mission Leader]] to make a decision.

[[Mission Leader]] talks to [[MC]], "Take the deal"

[[MC]] says "Deal. Where is the weapon?"

[[Researcher]] asks "Why should I trust you?" The [[MC]] still doesn't move, and stays silent.

[[Researcher]] stutters "I-it's on a transport vehicle, they wrecked or ran into some rogues, I think-"

[[MC]] commands "Coordinates," remaining steady.

[[Researcher]] looks down to a tablet on a desk next to him, taps a few times, then asks "Ready?", looking to the [[MC]]. The [[MC]] does not respond.

[[Researcher]] looks back down and says, "(Some planetary coordinates), it's just inside the first ring," then looks back to the [[MC]].

[[MC]] says "Wait," a moment passes, [[Mission Leader]] confirms "Satellite shows a wreckage, fresh. Target is right, some rogue bots must've hit them, they're still hanging around the area."

[[MC]] says "Alright, I'm going to give you a radio, so we can stay in touch," the [[Researcher]] says "Ok."

[[MC]] takes one hand off his weapon, slowly, showing his empty hand, but keeping his weapon trained on the [[Researcher]], and takes a device from his back left side. The [[MC]] is moving slowly and intentionally because he takes the threat of [[Researcher]] seriously.

[[MC]] sets the device on a rock next to him, then says "Come and get it once I'm gone. Press the button to turn it on." as he repositions his hand back onto the weapon.

The [[Researcher]] says "Okay. And then what?"

[[MC]] lowers his weapon, and then looks slowly to the elevator, then looks back to the [[Researcher]], turns around, and walks into the elevator, then turns to look back at the [[Researcher]].

As the [[MC]] turns around, the [[Animal]] appears and enters the elevator.

Shot cuts inside the elevator, where the [[MC]] is standing and the [[Animal]] is looking at him.

The [[Researcher]] starts to say "That's a-" before the doors close and cut him off.
