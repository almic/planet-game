Made for private security guards to defend local facilities. Light, small, large magazine, automatic firing.

# Function
Can focus on weak-points to rapidly destroy multiple vulnerable machines without reloading. Also effective damage for groups of weak machines. Higher velocity rounds than the side arm, can damage heavily armored machines.
