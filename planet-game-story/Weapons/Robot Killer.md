Some kind of tool used on planet. Happens to be very good at destroying robots.

# Function
Common tool on the planet, ammunition is abundant. Fires electrified metal pellets. Charged shots can disable machines temporarily, or instantly destroy small machines. Repeated regular shots can destroy machines as if frying delicate internal units.
